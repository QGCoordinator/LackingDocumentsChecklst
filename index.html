INSTRUCTIONS GOING TO PAG-IBIG

  1. Get Member's Data Form at the "Registration" Counter.
    Ask the PAG-IBIG Personnel for a priority number. Go to the Registration Counter and ask the personnel for a printed Member's Data Form.
  2. Make sure your current Company and Membership Status are updated.
    Check all the details in your Member's Data Form carefully. If any information is outdated or incorrect, ask for a Member's Change Information Form and update the necessary details before proceeding.
  3. Ask the personnel assigned at Registration to connect you to "Housing Loan".
    Once your Member's Data Form is complete and your details are confirmed to be updated, inform the Registration Counter personnel that you are applying for a Housing Loan and ask them to direct you to the Housing Loan counter.
  4. Prepare your Member's Status Verification Slip and Borrower's Validation Sheet.
    Make sure that you bring the forms before going to the Housing Loan counter. Prepare a photocopy of your valid ID, preferably your Loyalty Card, to be certified seen of ID in the Housing Loan counter. For Attorney in Fact (AIF), they also need to prepare a photocopy of their valid ID.
  5. Make sure the "Name of Developer" and "Subdivision Project Where Unit is to be Purchased" are correctly filled in.
    In the Borrower's Validation Sheet, write 8990 HOUSING DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION as the Name of Developer, then choose the correct project and write your Block and Lot number:
    • DECA HOMES LEGANES RESIDENCES SITE (2, 3, OR 5)   BLOCK __________ LOT __________
    • DECA HOMES PAVIA RESORT RESIDENCES (PHASE 1, 2, OR 3)   BLOCK __________ LOT __________
    • DECA HOMES STA. BARBARA RESORT RESIDENCES (PHASE 1, 2, OR 3)   BLOCK __________ LOT __________
